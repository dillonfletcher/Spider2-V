This task tests the use of job completion trigger.
1. Click "Deploy" in the upper panel.
2. Choose "Jobs".
3. Click "Create job", and choose "Deploy job".
4. In job settings section, set the job name to "Sales_Summary".
5. In triggers section, enable "Run when another job finishes".
6. Set "Project" as "Christmas", "Job" as "Sales_Process", "Completes on" as "Success".
7. Click "Save".
