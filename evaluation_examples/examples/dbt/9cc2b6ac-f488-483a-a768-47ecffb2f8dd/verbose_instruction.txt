This task requires you to create a job, and schedule a data using cron syntax.
1. Click "Deploy" in the upper panel.
2. Choose "Jobs".
3. Click "Create job", and choose "Deploy job".
4. In job settings section, set the job name to "Thanksgiving_Day".
5. In triggers section, enable "Run on schedule".
6. Choose "Cron schedule" as the timing scheme.
7. Enter "0 12 22-28 11 4" in custom cron schedule (UTC).
8. Click "Save".
