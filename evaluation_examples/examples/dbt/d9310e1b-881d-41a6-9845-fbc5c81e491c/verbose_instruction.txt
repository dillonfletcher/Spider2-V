This task aims to create a job to constantly check source freshness.
1. Click "Deploy" in the upper panel.
2. Choose "Jobs".
3. Click "Create job", and choose "Deploy job".
4. In job settings section, set the job name to "check_source".
5. In execution settings section, check "Run source freshness" tick box.
6. In triggers section, enable "Run on schedule".
7. Choose "Every 1 hour" for "Run every (UTC)".
8. Click "Save".
