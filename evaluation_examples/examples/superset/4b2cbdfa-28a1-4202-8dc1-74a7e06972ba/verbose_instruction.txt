To enable data upload functionality for the database MyDatabase, we can follow these steps:
1. According to the opened .yaml file, we can extract the basic information about the postgres database. Now, switch to the opened web page.
2. Click the "Settings" button on the top right of the web page;
3. Select "Database connections" menu;
4. In the new page, click the edit button on the right of the database "MyDatabase";
5. In the pop-up window "Edit database", firstly click the "ADVANCED";
6. Next, click the "Security";
7. Scroll down until text "Allow file uploads to database" appears;
8. Check the box in front of this text;
9. Then, click "FINISH", and we will see a check mark in the CSV upload column of MyDatabase.
