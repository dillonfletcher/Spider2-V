To see the Audit log of deleting actions by the user whose ID is admin in ascending order of logical date, we can follow the steps:
1. Hover over the 'Browse' menu in the top menu bar in the webpage until the corresponding dropdown menu appears;
2. Click the Audit Logs in the dropdown menu;
3. Click search;
4. Add filter of event 'delete';
5. Add filter of owner id 'admin';
6. Click the Logical Date in the table header
