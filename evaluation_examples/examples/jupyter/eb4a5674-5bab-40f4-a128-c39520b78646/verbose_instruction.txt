This task is to get the history of the commands. On the opened jupyter notebook:
1. Scroll down to the bottom, hover your mouse over the blank space at the bottom.
2. Click button "Click to add a cell."
3. Copy the code below to this cell:
```
with open('history.txt', 'w') as file:
    file.write('\n'.join(In))
```
4. Click the button "Run" in the menu bar on the top.
5. In the drop-down menu, select and click "Run All Cells".
6. Wait for about 10 seconds, there will be a history.txt on the left side panel.
7. Double click "history.txt" on the left side panel.
8. Scroll down to the bottom, delete history related commands. In this instruction, you need to delete:
```
with open('history.txt', 'w') as file:
    file.write('\n'.join(In))
```
9. Save history.txt.
