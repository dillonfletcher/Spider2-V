To complete the visualization, please follow these steps:

1. Click the "New" button on the top right;
2. Choose "Question" in the drop-down panel;
3. Choose "Products" in the "Sample Database" drop-down panel;
4. Under "Summarize" choose "Sum of Price";
5. After "by", choose "Category";
6. Click on the plus sign after the "Category" and choose "Created At - Quarter";
7. Click on Visualize;
8. Click on Visualization -> Bar;
9. Click on Bar again to pull out the Bar Options panel;
10. Under Display - Stacking, choose Stack;
11. Click Done;
12. Click the "Download full results" button (cloud icon) on the bottom right;
13. Choose ".png" in the panel;
14. Open the local path "/home/user/Downloads";
15. Rename the visualization file as "stack_chart.png".
