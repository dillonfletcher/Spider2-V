To complete the visualization, please follow these steps:

1. Click the "New" button on the top right;
2. Choose "Question" in the drop-down panel;
3. Choose "Products" in the "Sample Database" drop-down panel;
4. Under "Summarize", click "Pick the metric you want to see";
5. Choose "Sum of ..."
6. Click "Pick a column to group by"
7. Then Click "Price"
8. After "by", Click "Pick a column to group by"
9. Click "Category";
10. Click on the plus sign after the "Category" and choose "Created At - Quarter";
11. Click on Visualize;
12. Click on Visualization -> Bar;
13. Click on Bar again to pull out the Bar Options panel;
14. Under Display - Stacking, choose Stack;
15. Click Done;
16. Click the "Download full results" button (cloud icon) on the bottom right;
17. Choose ".png" in the panel;
18. Open the local path "/home/user/Downloads";
19. Rename the visualization file as "stack_chart.png".
