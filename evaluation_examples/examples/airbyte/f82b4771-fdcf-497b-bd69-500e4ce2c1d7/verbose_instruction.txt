In this task, we want to change the sync mode of the existing connection from "full_refresh | Overwrite" to "full_refresh | Append": 
1. Switch to the browser window;
2. In the opened connection page of Airbyte, click the link of the existing connection from Postgres to Postgres on the right;
3. After that, in the connection configuration page, locate the "Sync Mode" column in the "activate the streams you want to sync" table, and change the "full_refresh | Overwrite" choice to "full_refresh | Append" for all streams.
4. Click the "Set up connection" button in the bottom right corner to complete the setting changes.
