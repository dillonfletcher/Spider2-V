This task is to get data through Marketplace on Snowflake. In the opened Snowsight web page,
1. Click "Data Products >> Marketplace" sequentially.
2. In the search box with "Search providers and data products" prompt on the top of the window, type in "American".
3. Hit Enter.
4. In the second row and second column of the search results, click on the result named "American Community Survey, 2016".
5. Click the "Get" button on the top-right.
6. Click the "Get" button on the bottom of the pop-up window.
7. After a few seconds, a success window pops up. Click "Done" button.
